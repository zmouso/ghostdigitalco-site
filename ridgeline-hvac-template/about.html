The Ridgeline Story

Owner James Whitfield left a large HVAC company because he was tired of watching customers get oversold and overcharged. He took his certification, bought a used service van, and started doing things the right way.

Word spread fast in Farragut and West Knoxville. By 2015, Ridgeline had a full crew of certified technicians and a reputation built on neighbors telling neighbors.
